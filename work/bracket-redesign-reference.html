Tap any match to expand its day-by-day scores · tap again to collapse.
Behaviour: round tabs switch which round's matches are listed (default to the live/current round). Each match card is tappable; tapping expands it inline (accordion — one open at a time) to a per-day points grid for both players (columns = the round window's days, cells = that day's points, the daily leader highlighted, plus a Total). Your match is highlighted, auto-expandable, and shows "Log to win your match". A "full bracket" zoom-out tree can be a secondary toggle. No schema change — scores come from the existing contest_matches + community_entries.
