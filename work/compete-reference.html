Compete — one hub for 1v1, tournaments, and teams

1v1, tournaments, and teams are the same thing: a time-boxed contest over points inside a community. One "Compete" hub (the challenge feed) lists every contest you're in; a single "+ New contest" picks the format; each format has its own detail view. All scoring reuses your existing community points.
