Pointwell — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Pointwell ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal habit and reward-tracking application. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Pointwell you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information you provide, such as your name, handle, email address, and profile/avatar image.
  • Activity you log in the app — habits, entries, amounts, notes, photos, points, streaks, and the communities or systems you join.
  • Connected device data, only if you choose to link a wearable. If you connect WHOOP, we access the specific metrics you authorize (for example recovery, sleep, cycles/strain, workouts, and basic profile) to update your tracked rules. If you connect Google Health / Fitbit, we access the activity, fitness, and sleep metrics you authorize. We only request the scopes needed to power the features you use.
  • Basic technical data needed to operate the service, such as authentication tokens for your linked accounts.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core features: logging activity, computing points and streaks, showing progress, and powering communities, leaderboards, and challenges you take part in.
  • To sync data from a wearable you have connected into the rules you have set up.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the app.

How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only in these ways:

  • With other members of communities you join — for example your name, avatar, posts you choose to share, and your points on shared leaderboards. Content you keep private (a private log) is not posted to any feed.
  • With service providers that help us run the app, including our hosting and database provider (Supabase) and the wearable providers you connect (WHOOP, Google/Fitbit), which process your request under their own terms and privacy policies.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored using Supabase (Postgres) with row-level security controls intended to keep private data private. Access tokens for connected devices are stored server-side and are not exposed to other users. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You can disconnect a linked device at any time from the app's Integrations settings, which stops further syncing. To delete your account or request deletion of your data, contact us at the email below and we will remove your information from our systems, subject to any legal retention requirements.

Your choices

  • Connecting a wearable is optional and always requires your explicit authorization on the provider's consent screen.
  • You can revoke access at any time from the app, and from your WHOOP or Google/Fitbit account settings.
  • You control what you share publicly versus keep as a private log.

Children's privacy

Pointwell is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region), and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
